Straight answers

The questions you're already asking.

Can't I just remove it from Chrome?

Yes — and we'd never claim otherwise. Any extension can be removed from your browser. Pact's job isn't to imprison you; it's to add enough friction that a 2-second impulse can't override a decision you made deliberately. That honest friction is the whole product.

Is my data private?

Completely. Pact has no servers and no tracking. Your blocklist and your streak live on your device. There's nothing to sell, because there's nowhere for it to go.

What if I have a real emergency?

You choose your own friction when you commit. A cooldown or a typed challenge lets you get through when you truly need to — deliberately, not on reflex. You're never actually trapped.

Will it block apps on my phone?

Not yet. Pact is a Chrome extension today — great for desktop. Real strictness on mobile is the next build, and we'll be honest about what each platform can and can't do.

How much does it cost?

The basics are free forever. Pro will be available monthly, yearly, or as a one-time lifetime unlock — final prices aren't set yet. Join the list for founding-member terms.

Is this medical treatment?

No. Pact is a focus and commitment tool, not therapy or a medical device, and it doesn't diagnose or treat anything. If you're struggling, please talk to a professional — Pact can sit alongside that, not replace it.
